Supporter

Nextdoor is the free and private social network for neighborhoods used by more than 39,000 neighborhoods across the country. On Nextdoor, neighbors create private neighborhood websites where they can ask questions, get to know one another, and exchange local advice and recommendations. Nextdoor also partners with city agencies, like police and fire departments and city hall. Cities utilize Nextdoor to enhance communications between the city and residents as well as to establish virtual neighborhood watch. Nextdoor's mission is to use the power of technology to help residents and cities build stronger, safer places to call home.
