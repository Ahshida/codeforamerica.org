Announcing the 2015 Summit

The 2015 Code for America will kick-off September 30 in Oakland, California. For three days, more than 1,200 civic technologists will take over the Oakland Marriott City Center, rolling up sleeves to delve into how, together, we can transform government for the 21st Century.
