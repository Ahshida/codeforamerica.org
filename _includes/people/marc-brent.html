Marc Brent

Major Gifts Lead

Member of

  • Staff

Marc Brent is a Boston native who relocated to San Francisco in the spring of 2014, joining Code for America in December 2014 in the role of Major Gifts Lead. Prior to this, Marc spent three years at the Harvard Kennedy School in Cambridge, MA, where he led fundraising efforts for a number of research centers and initiatives. Marc brings to CfA a wide-range of experience with advisory boards including domestic and international donors. Having worked extensively with supporters who care deeply about the intersection of government and civic engagement, Marc has observed that the most powerful changes occur when people in their communities are empowered to create them. He is excited to learn, collaborate and network with CfA's community of first-rate change agents to expand the organization's far reaching impact.
