Shelly Ni

Pittsburgh Team

Member of

  • 2015 Fellow

Shelly is an interaction designer — someone who solves problems that happen over time. As a co-founder of Propel, she designed a food stamp application that works on any smartphone. For her MFA thesis, she created guides to apply for social security cards and state IDs. She runs a feminist superhero underwear line, and holds design degrees from the School of Visual Arts and Stanford.
