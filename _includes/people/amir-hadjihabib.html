Amir Hadjihabib

2015 Fellow

Member of

Amir is a San Francisco based Product Designer and avid photographer. At Zendesk, he was part of the team that created the acclaimed Link-SF app, a tool for connecting the underserved community with life-saving resources. He is a professor of User Experience at the Academy of Art University. Amir seeks inspiration from the outdoors and can often be found feeding his voracious cookie habit.
