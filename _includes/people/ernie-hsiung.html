Ernie is a front-end developer and a community organizer with an interest in both actual and virtual communities. Born and raised in the Bay Area and living in Miami, he most recently served as a software developer at Rackspace and was co-captain of Code for Miami, a local Code for America civic hacking brigade. He holds a BS in Computer Science Engineering from UC Davis.
