Mateo Clarke is a Web Developer, Fiddler, and Community Organizer dedicated to leveraging technology for social good. Mateo works as a Frontend Engineer using Javascript & Ruby on Rails at AcademicWorks, helping schools and foundations award more scholarship money to students.

As Brigade Captain at Open Austin, Mateo has helped to activate the volunteer civic hacking community in Austin through developer outreach, partnerships with municipal and governmental groups, and running civic hack nights. Mateo currently serves on the Austin Community Technology and Telecommunications Commission, which advises the Austin City Council on technology matters.
