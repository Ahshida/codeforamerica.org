Be a part of the biggest Code for America Summit yet! We're looking for a diverse group of speakers to share their stories about transforming government for the 21st century. In particular, we're looking for champions in the field who are:

  • Making digital services more dignified for users
  • Creating collaborative and inclusive interfaces to government
  • Engaging the community in new ways
  • Transforming organizational culture
  • Bringing data-driven, user-centered, and iterative approaches to government processes
  • Working on the cutting edge of safety and justice, economic development, health, and government operations
  • Building new tools and platforms for government

We'll be accepting submissions until June 1, 2015, and reviewing submissions on a rolling basis.If you have any questions, please reach out to Debs Schrimmer, Summit Conference Content Coordinator, debs@codeforamerica.org

Tips:
  • Breakout Sessions should be actionable. Focus on designing a session that lets participants walk away with new skills to put into action.
  • Lightning Talks should focus on storytelling. Show, don't tell. What's your story? What are the bigger ideas or lessons that it demonstrates?
  • We like sessions that reflect our Principles for 21st Century Governments.
