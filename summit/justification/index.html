Justification to Attend: 5th Annual Code for America Summit

Hosted by Code for America

September 30 - October 2, 2015

Oakland Marriott City Center – Oakland, CA

To: [Manager Name]
From: [Your Name]

Re: Approval to Attend the 2015 Code for America Summit

Dear [Manager],

I am requesting approval to attend the Code for America Summit, September 30 - October 2 in Oakland, CA for the discounted rate of $495.

The 2015 Code for America Summit provides [your agency] employees with an inspiring and cost-effective opportunity to further develop the leadership and technology skills that are central to the development of government leaders at [your agency]. Attending the Summit is an excellent opportunity to share, publicize and educate civic technology leaders on the capabilities and merits of [your city].

The Code for America Summit is the preeminent annual gathering of civic technology leaders — ranging from mayors to entrepreneurs, community organizers to Chief Technology Officers. The Summit gives [your city] an opportunity to generate and strengthen private and non-governmental partnerships which further community engagement planning and development for [your agency].

I believe that this experience will be the highest value for dollar professional development experience I’ll have this year. What I learn, and the peer network I will cultivate, will enable me to be more effective as well as support me in my role in an ongoing manner.

Details:

  • Date: September 30 - October 2, 2015
  • Pricing and Registration Contact: summit@codeforamerica.org
  • Billing/Invoicing Address: 155 9th St. San Francisco CA, 94103
  • Tax ID/EIN: 27-1067272
  • Cost: Government Staff Price (me) $495; Non-Government Price $1495

The Summit is filled with three days of keynote sessions, panel discussions, breakout sessions, and interactive problem-solving workgroup. The 2015 program includes:

  1. Peer to Peer Presentations: Code for America’s eight city partners will accompany their respective fellows to discuss the achieved accomplishments.
  2. Transforming 21st Century Government: Each day includes talks from industry leaders and those working to develop the tools for 21st century government. Highlighted speakers include:
    • Mayor Libby Schaaf, City of Oakland
    • Will Lightbourne, Director of the California Department of Social Services
    • Dana Chisnell, CEO of Usability Works and Generalist Problem Solver, United States Digital Service
  3. Tech Fair: An opportunity to explore cutting edge solutions that improve public service and citizen experience.
  4. Educational Breakouts and Training Workshops: More than 40 sessions across 6 tracks provide attendees with learning opportunities across a variety of topic areas:
    • Design for people's needs: Learn how to understand and prioritize the needs of citizens who rely on key public services.
    • Make it easy for everyone to participate: Learn how other governments build inclusive government services for a true cross section of the community.
    • Focus on what government can do: Learn how to prioritize work where government can have the most impact and adopting a platform approach that others can build on top of.
    • Make data easy to find and use: Learn how to adopt open data policies that support transparency and help foster the broader civic technology ecosystem.
    • Use data to make and improve decisions: Learn ways to embrace analytics, predictive modeling and data-informed performance management to improve efficiency.
    • Choose the right technology for the job: Learn how other governments are reforming procurement policies to make it easier to buy better technology.
    • Organize for results: Learn how to improve internal workflows, teaming structures, and new approaches to problem solving and service delivery.

I will submit a post-conference report for your review, and include possible action items with recommendations/improvements for our program. More information regarding the Code for America Summit, including registration, costs, and session descriptions, may be viewed at: http://codeforamerica.org/summit/.

Best,
[NAME] [TITLE, DEPARTMENT]
