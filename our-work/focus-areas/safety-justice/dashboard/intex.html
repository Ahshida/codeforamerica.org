About the Jail Dashboard

Originally built with Louisville in partnership with the Laura and John Arnold Foundation to address jail overcrowding and provide insight into the jail population profile, the dashboard helps the jail administrator, judges and other stakeholders understand the conditions in the metro jail and use this data to visualize how their decisions - at both the individual and policy level - affect program, facility and inmate outcomes.

The Criminal Justice Commission and Jail Policy Committee have been wanting a tool like this since 1980.

BETH NIBLOCK, Former Chief Information Officer,Louisville, KY

The Product

Features enable stakeholders to: (1) visualize and compare the existing capacity of the jail and of alternatives to incarceration programs, so that judges can better decide where to place each defendant; (2) track who is where, across the entire jail population and; (3) understand population trends, as well as filter the data by gender, age, court, sentence status or length of stay.
