Apply to bring the fellows to you

The Code for America Fellowship is a highly competitive program. We are only able to accept a handful of local governments each year. To express interest, the primary point of contact must identify an executive sponsor (e.g. mayor, city manager, county administrator, public health director, or equivalent) and complete the interest form below. Following the completion of the form, you will receive information about the rest of the application process.

Final applications for the 2016/17 program will be due by April 29th, 2016.
