September 30 - October 2, Oakland, California: Be a part of the biggest Code for America Summit yet! We're looking for a wide range of speakers to share their stories about transforming government for the 21st century. If selected, speakers get a complimentary ticket to the Summit. We want to feature champions in the field who are:

  • Making digital government services more usable and accessible
  • Helping governments and communities collaborate in new ways
  • Transforming the organizational culture and practices of government
  • Applying data-driven, user-centered, and iterative approaches to civic challenges
  • Making real impact in the areas of safety and justice, economic development, health, and/or government operations

We'll be accepting proposals until June 1, 2015, and reviewing submissions on a rolling basis. If you have any questions, please reach out to Debs Schrimmer, Summit Conference Content Coordinator (debs@codeforamerica.org).

Tips:
  • If selected, speakers get a complimentary ticket to the Summit.
  • Breakouts are 1-hour, focused sessions with a group of 20-50 participants. These sessions should be actionable and focused on doing. Tell us how participants will walk away from your session with new skills to put into action.
  • Lightning Talks are 5-minute, rapid-fire talks on the main stage. These sessions should focus on showing, not telling. What did you build or do? Why does it matter?
  • We like sessions that reflect our Practices of 21st Century Governments.
