Miami-Dade County

In addition to trade and tourism, Miami-Dade County has a fast-growing technology sector and is eager to engage their tech talent in service of civic goals. In 2015, Code for America Fellows will work with the County to improve economic development and regulatory outcomes, enriching feedback loops and improving access to services with the goal of connecting local entrepreneurs to government, and building tools to streamline and improve how residents start and grow their businesses.
