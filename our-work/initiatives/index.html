Health & Human Services

Serving Vulnerable Residents

Improving access to necessary health and human services is critical to ensuring the safety of our populations. Code for America is working on software to make it easier for people to sign up for food stamps, data standards for restaurant and home safety inspections, and policy changes to allow governments to communicate more effectively with clients via SMS.

Procurement

The Foundation for Change

Government purchasing rules have fallen out of step with the pace at which technology now improves, and almost make it inevitable that local governments will be poorly served by their vendors. We are working to shift this dynamic by piloting new models that give governments more control over the purchasing process, more flexibility in terms of options, and bring new vendors into the ecosystem, while still providing important protections against fraud and abuse.
