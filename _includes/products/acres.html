Acres

Acres connects farmers to the information they need to start an urban farm in West Sacramento.

Created by

  • Imanol Aranzadi
  • Natasha Fountain
  • Grant Smith

Finding land for urban farming can be difficult. Acres is a tool that surface the municipal data like zoning regulations, water billing data, and soil characteristics in a farmer friendly manner. The tool provides farmers with the information they need and connects them with landowners. The city, or any private landowner, can take the first step in creating an urban farm by drawing a future farm site on the map, so that all information about that property is immediately available. Then, the farmer directly connects with the landowner, negotiates a lease agreement, and starts sowing seeds soon thereafter.
