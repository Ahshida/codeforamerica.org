Step 1: Basic Information

Apply for the 2016 Fellowship

Use your skills for good. Code for America's Fellowship program pairs teams of technologists with local governments to develop digital tools that help cities and counties deliver key public services across health, economic development, and safety and justice. Fellows are based in San Francisco for one year. Express your interest right now. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis starting in May through July 15th. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out.

  • If you're not a Brigade member, you can leave this blank.

Application Steps
There are 3 steps in the application process — please follow the guide below to see where you are.

  • Basic Information

    Quick questions about you, your skill set, and your background (5 minutes)

  • Written Responses

    In-depth questions about your interest in the fellowship. (15-30 minutes)

  • Skills Challenge

    A 20-30 minute task that tests your chosen skillset.
