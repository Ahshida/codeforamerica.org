EBT Near Me (Alpha)

EBTNearMe is the easiest way to find stores and surcharge-free ATMs where you can use your EBT card in California.

Created by

  • Rhys Fureigh
  • Alan Williams
  • Jacob Solomon

EBTNearMe is the easiest way to find stores and surcharge-free ATMs where you can use your EBT card in California. We built it because California welfare recipients pay nearly $20 million per year in ATM surcharge fees partially because there isn't an easy way to find the free ones.

It's an open source project built with public retailer data from the USDA and ATM data graciously shared by the CA Office of Systems Integration.

FAQ

How do people do it now?
Currently EBT users can use the FNS retail locator or the CA EBT website to find stores that accept EBT cards. The San Francisco Human Services Agency created this surcharge-free ATM locator but the rest of California relies on this PDF document.

Why California?
Because we're based in San Francisco and we know people in California. That's it. Please get in touch with health-lab@codeforamerica.org if you think EBTNearMe would be a useful tool in your community and want to work together to make it happen.

What does Alpha mean?
Alpha means we are still validating the feasibility and value of the project so things will change. Please don't count on this working the same way tomorrow. Once it's stable enough to use we'll upgrade it to Beta.
