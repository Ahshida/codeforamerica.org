  • Schedule

    The Summit is three days of action-packed content, featuring passionate people who are transforming government.

    Each morning opens with main stage talks, and afternoons feature more intimate breakouts, the unconference and hackathon, and the Tech Fair.

    See the online schedule

  • Live Stream

    Main stage talks will be live streamed on the Summit homepage each morning starting at 9 am.

    Follow along with live video on this page, and keep up-to-date with the conversation on Twitter using the hashtag #CfASummit.

  • Venue

    The Summit is hosted at the Oakland Marriott City Center, in downtown Oakland. Get directions to the hotel.

    The Marriott is just a block from 12st Oakland BART, with quick access to regional airports and downtown San Francisco.
