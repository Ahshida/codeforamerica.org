Gabrielle Jones

Executive Coordinator

Member of

  • Staff

Gabrielle Jones joined Code for America in October of 2014. Originally from Tampa, Florida, she received her Master’s of Social Work degree from the University of Washington in Seattle. Throughout her varied career, Gabrielle has worked in several social service settings conducting health education, outreach, case management, and community building activities. In Florida, she worked to increase immigrant and refugee children’s access to affordable health insurance. As a project coordinator at Big Brothers Big Sisters of Tampa Bay, Gabrielle matched at-risk children to adult mentors. Most recently, Gabrielle worked at Golden Gate Regional Center providing case management to over 90 individuals with developmental disabilities. Gabrielle joined Code for America in the hopes of using her diverse knowledge to influence system-wide change.
