Farm Stand

Farm Stand is a tool that helps urban farms reach potential customers in West Sacramento.

Created by

  • Imanol Aranzadi
  • Natasha Fountain
  • Grant Smith

Once a farm is established, it needs customers. Farm Stand is a collection of tools that urban farmers in West Sacramento can use to easily catalogue what they were growing, when it will be available, where they are selling their produce, and for how much. Residents can sign up to receive either texts or emails, and farmers have a simple mobile interface for updating their available produce before messages are sent to everyone who signed up for them.
