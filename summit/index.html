#About

Announcing the 2015 Summit

When: September 30 - October 2
Where: Oakland Marriott City Center

The 2015 Code for America will kick-off September 30 in Oakland, California. For three days, more than 1,300 government leaders, technologists, and community members will take over the Oakland Marriott City Center, to delve into how, together, we can transform government for the 21st Century.

Speakers on tap

Speakers include Mayor Libby Schaaf (City of Oakland), Kellan Elliott-McCrea (CTO, Etsy), Dana Chisnell (CEO of Usability Works and Generalist Problem Solver, United States Digital Service), Susan Shaheen (UC Berkeley), Gabe Klein (Mobility and Tech advisor), and Emily Castor (Director of Community Relations, Lyft).
