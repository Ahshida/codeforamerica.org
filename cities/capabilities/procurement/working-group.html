Procurement Working Group Details

What do I need to do as part of the working group?

To join the Working Group, you'll need to identify two designated staff members to participate â€” one from a technology or innovation-focused department, and one from the procurement or legal department. Both participants will be expected to:

  • Participate in 2-3 preparatory phone calls with CfA staff and/or advisors before September 2014 to help us understand needs and priorities around procurement in your municipality
  • Engage in 1-2 collaborative virtual roundtables with other cities to share ideas, give feedback, and learn from each other's approaches.
  • Attend the CfA Summit (September 23-25 in San Francisco) and participate in 2-3 hours of working sessions on procurement

These are the minimum participation expectations, and we hope to work closely with our working group partners to go above and beyond what's listed here.
