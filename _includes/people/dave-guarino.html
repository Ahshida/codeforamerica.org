Dave Guarino

Member of

Dave Guarino is a software engineer focused on increasing access and dignity in the social safety net. His primary interests these days include duct-taping programmatic APIs onto legacy government interfaces, data processing/ETL, and strategies for analytic rigor in early-stage product development. In a former life, Dave was an analyst in the health care sector. He was also a CfA fellow in 2013. Dave is a graduate of UC Berkeley and lives in Oakland, California.
