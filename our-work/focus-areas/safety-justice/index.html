The criminal justice system in the United States is in need of a redesign. Our prisons and jails are overcrowded, recidivism rates are high, and police relations with the community are strained. Code for America works with local governments to create justice systems that are safe, fair and effective. We use data and technology to improve criminal justice data, connect people to the right interventions, and strenghten community/police relations.

What We're Working On

Real-time Analysis of Jail Conditions

The Jail Population Management Dashboard gives judges, corrections staff, and police a real-time, in-depth view of the Louisville metro jail system. With unprecedented access to jail data, stakeholders are able to visualize how their decisions affect outcomes, discover trends, and coordinate efforts across agencies.

Reducing Failures to Appear with Text Messages

Courtbot is a simple web service for handling court case data. It enables city residents who receive traffic tickets to text their citation number to get information on how to pay the ticket or get information and reminders about their court dates.
