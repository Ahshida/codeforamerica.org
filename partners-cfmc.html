Mexico City Government Pioneers Innovation to Improve People’s Lives



Mexico City’s Laboratorio Para la Ciudad (Government Innovation Lab) recruited, trained and guided five skilled technologist to serve as fellows for a yearlong program in which they partnered with Mexico City government departments. Working in partnership with their government departments, they used technology to devise solutions to problems facing city residents.
