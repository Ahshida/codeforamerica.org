Watch the videos

All the 2015 Code for America Summit main stage talks are streaming now. Dive into some highlighted talks below, or browse all the talks.

  • Jake Solomon – Defining a 21st Century Government

    The Code for America Health team is taking a user-centered approach to understanding bottlenecks in the CalFresh (food stamps) enrollment process and identifying high priority areas for improvement — with implications for underlying policy and process.

  • Sam Vaughn – A New Standard for Community Engagement

    Sam Vaughn, Neighborhood Change Agent, Office of Neighborhood Safety, City of Richmond, CA, describes how his team is meeting community members where they are and creating lasting impact in safety and justice with the Operation Peacemaker Fellowship.

  • Dana Chisnell – Next Generation of Civic Designers

    Dana Chisnell, User Researcher at the United States Digital Service, spotlights how public servants are civic designers in their own right, and the importance of shifting the conversation from "how do we get government to learn about design" to "how do we use design to solve problems."
