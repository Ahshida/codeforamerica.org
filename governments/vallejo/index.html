Vallejo

The Bay Area city of Vallejo has one of the most diverse population of any city in the United States. It was hit hard by the foreclosure crisis and is still recovering from municipal bankruptcy. In this context of severely limited resources and reduced staff, Vallejo is partnering with Code for America to use technology and innovation to improve public safety. With a combination of data analytics and civic engagement, the Code for America fellows will address the trust between law enforcement and Vallejo residents.

“The Code for America fellowship is an opportunity for us to enhance communication and increase engagement, and to maximize public involvement and collaboration. The City Council and I are thrilled to have been selected after having made the application to Code for America a top priority this year. We look forward to the many possibilities that will surely come from this fantastic partnership.”

Mayor Osby Davis, Vallejo, California
